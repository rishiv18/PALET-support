How to Play Bento Blocks

Objective

Place all your pieces on the board to score points. The player with the highest score wins!

Rules

  • Each player takes turns placing one piece at a time
  • Pieces must connect corner-to-corner with your other pieces
  • Pieces cannot overlap or touch edge-to-edge with your other pieces
  • Your first piece must touch a corner of the board
  • Score points equal to the number of squares in each piece you place

Controls

  • Click on a piece to select it
  • Click on the board to place the selected piece
  • Right-click a piece to rotate it
  • Double-click a piece to flip it
