PALET User Privacy Policy

18/09/2025

Privacy First: PALET keeps your corporate inventory data secure and private. Your business information stays on your device under your control.

What Information Does PALET Access?

Inventory Data: Product names, quantities, EAN codes, and descriptions you enter

Images: Product photos you add to your inventory

Camera: Only for QR code scanning and product photography

Device Storage: To save your inventory data locally on your device

How Your Data is Protected

  • Local Storage Only: All data stays on your device
  • No Cloud Uploads: We don't automatically upload your data anywhere
  • Encrypted Storage: Data is stored using iOS/Android security standards
  • You Control Exports: Only you decide when to share data via CSV export

Permissions We Request

Camera Access: "Allow PALET to access your camera to scan QR codes"

Photo Library: "This app needs access to photo library to select product images"

What We Don't Do

  • Sell your business data to third parties
  • Track your personal activities outside the app
  • Store your data on external servers without your consent
  • Access other apps or personal files on your device

Corporate Compliance

PALET is designed to help businesses maintain data privacy compliance. Your inventory data processing remains under your organization's control.

Updates

We'll notify you of any privacy policy changes through the app. Continued use indicates acceptance of updated terms.

Questions?
